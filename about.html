Who We Are

Coffee Shop is a natural step of growth for Red Rooster Coffee. We’re excited to offer the finest coffee strains available. Our lab and cafe is housed with in the Red Rooster building at 823 E. Main St. Swing by the new cafe to taste what we’ve created.

Our Story

Created in 2016 as an answer to the demand from cafes and restaurants for a high-quality coffee source, Red Rooster Coffee Roaster developed Swallowtail. Nestled in the tiny hamlet of Floyd, Virginia, Swallowtail has met that need by working with high-end coffee importers and local farmers.

Our Values

At Swallowtail, quality is not just a word, it is the cornerstone of our business. Every step, from importing to tasting to blending and packaging is all done with the intent of providing our customer with the highest quality product. We inspect and cup every batch of coffee we receive at our lab in Floyd and taste them religiously throughout their time in our care.
